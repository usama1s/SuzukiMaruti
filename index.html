About Fort Point Maruti-Suzuki Services

We started our journey with a Hero Honda dealership in the year 1993. In the same year, we were awarded the dealership of Hindustan Motors. Under the able guidance of our Managing Director Mr. Sundeep Kumar Bafna, we made the dealership gain a reputation of being one of the best service providers in automotive trade. Towards the end of the year 2000, our group had over 30,000 satisfied customers in its fold. It was at the same time that we started our journey in the small car segment with the dealership of Daewoo in Thane and later we switched over to the popular FIAT India dealership.

Three years later, in the year 2003, we were awarded the dealership of General Motors India Ltd. Within a span of just 3 years from then, our dealership was rated amongst the top three in all operational parameters viz. sales volume, after sales satisfaction, and spares operations.

In 2006, our Managing Director Mr. Sundeep Kumar Bafna, took a bold step ahead with a vision to cater to a wider range of customers and took the prestigious franchisee of Maruti Udyog Ltd. at three locations - Mahim, Mahalaxmi, and Thane.
